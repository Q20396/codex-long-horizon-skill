# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be welcoming, practical, and respectful. Contributors
are expected to collaborate in good faith, assume positive intent, and keep
feedback focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Sharing private information without permission
- Publishing secrets, client data, legal evidence, or confidential material
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests
- Pressuring maintainers to merge unsafe or unreviewed changes

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or
contributions that violate this standard. Maintainers may also temporarily or
permanently limit participation when needed to protect the project.

## Reporting

If there is no private maintainer contact published yet, ask the maintainers to
add an appropriate contact before sharing sensitive information. Do not post
private safety reports publicly.
