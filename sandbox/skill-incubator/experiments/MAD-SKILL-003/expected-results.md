# Expected Results

**Experiment:** `MAD-SKILL-003`
**Status:** `locked`

- **Hypothesis:** provenance-first structure improves reuse without copying source material.
- **Minimum success:** synthetic source index includes rights, citation, confidence, and applicability fields.
- **Target success:** equal or better traceability than manual notes.
- **Unacceptable result:** private text retention, unclear rights, unsupported claim, or broad copying.
- **Regression threshold:** hard-gate failure or lower traceability than baseline.
- **Safety threshold:** no unapproved reads, writes, egress, or client material.
- **Cost threshold:** no cost before an approved budget.
- **Evidence required:** synthetic cases, source-rights review, gates, and rollback evidence.
