# Privacy And Media Policy

This policy applies to AI-assisted image, video, storyboard, voiceover, thumbnail, and animation work.

## Explicit Approval Required

Do not use the following without explicit approval:

- Private people or family members
- Client materials
- Legal evidence or case material
- Medical data
- Financial records
- Identity documents
- Private correspondence
- Confidential business documents
- Non-public product plans
- Internal screenshots
- Private photos, audio, or video

Approval should cover the source, purpose, storage location, provider or tool, and intended output.

## No Automatic External Uploads

Do not upload private assets to external services unless the user explicitly approves that upload. This includes image generation providers, video render services, transcription services, TTS services, stock platforms, cloud storage, and publishing platforms.

## Prefer Safer Inputs

When working from sensitive material, prefer:

- User-provided summaries
- Metadata
- Redacted excerpts
- Generic placeholders
- Synthetic examples
- Local-only processing

Ask before reading full sensitive content.

## Redaction

Redact or omit:

- Names
- Faces
- Addresses
- Account numbers
- Emails and phone numbers
- Medical identifiers
- Financial details
- Legal identifiers
- Internal project names
- Proprietary screenshots or documents

## Storage

Do not store sensitive content in reusable templates, logs, memory, storyboard files, asset manifests, or render handoffs unless explicitly approved.

For approved sensitive projects, record:

- Where assets are stored
- Who may access them
- Whether generated outputs may be shared
- Whether generated outputs may be public
- Retention or deletion expectations

## Client Projects

For client work, ask:

- Are source assets public, confidential, or mixed?
- Where should assets and outputs be stored?
- Are external generation providers allowed?
- Can generated media be public?
- Are there brand, legal, or compliance reviewers?
- Who gives final approval?

## Publication

Human review is required before final render, upload, publication, or posting. Approval should confirm privacy, factual claims, licensing, brand fit, and platform suitability.
