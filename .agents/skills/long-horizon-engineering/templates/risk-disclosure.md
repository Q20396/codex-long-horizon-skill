# Risk Disclosure

Use this template for market, company, stock, sector, valuation, watchlist, or
financial research outputs.

Safety note: Do not include private portfolio data, account numbers, client
names, tax records, legal evidence, identity documents, API keys, or
confidential source content.

## Required Statement

This is data analysis only, not investment advice. It is not a recommendation
to buy, sell, hold, or trade any security. Verify current information and make
independent decisions before taking financial action.

## Important Limits

- Market prices, filings, news, and estimates may have changed after the
  analysis date.
- Data providers may have missing, delayed, revised, or permission-limited
  fields.
- Valuation depends on assumptions that may be wrong.
- Historical performance does not guarantee future results.
- Screens and watchlists identify research candidates, not buy lists.
- Personal suitability, tax impact, liquidity, risk tolerance, and portfolio
  context are not assessed unless explicitly provided and approved for review.
